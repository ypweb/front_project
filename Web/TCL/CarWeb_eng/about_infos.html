TCL Cartel Profile

TCL Cartel Co. Ltd is a first class subsidiary of TCL Group, specialized in developing & manufacturing & selling smart vehicle navigator and operating platform development, including fundamental vehicle service, community platform service, E-business service, online trading and etc. We provide a brand-new integration services to our auto consumers based on smart terminal, 3G high-speed wireless data and internet business content resources.

TCL Cartel has an industrial-leading independent R&D team, strongest manufacturing capabilities and high-class after-sale service. Through its continuous efforts and innovation, it will be a China’s biggest provider of TSP terminal device and service.

Action as lightning, intelligent life accompanying!

TCL Cartel devotes itself to “Creating Value for Customers & Creating Opportunities for Staff & Creating Benefits for the Society ”, insisting on guiding its long term development by intelligent future, carrying forward the “dedication, integrity, teamwork and innovation” spirit, deducing the “intelligent, practical and fashion” boutique concept, continuing to make business transformation and management innovation to enhance the core competitiveness of enterprises through the competitive strategy of “developing the best products, providing the best service and creating the best brand”.

Great ideals need a strong technology to realize, and only by this, TCL Cartel can bring our customers extraordinary products. TCL Cartel perfectly changes a traditional vehicle-mounted DVD/GPS to intelligent vehicle-mounted navigator by its original ideas, unique technology, humanized design and exquisite workmanship. Meanwhile, it creates an industry precedent and builds a real domestic Car Networking Era.
